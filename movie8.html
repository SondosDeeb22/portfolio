brief description Dunkirk is a war movie based on the real event of the Dunkirk evacuation during World War II, where hundreds of thousands of Allied soldiers were rescued from the beaches of Dunkirk, France, in 1940. This movie stands out for the methodologies they used to visualize the evacuation, telling the story from three different prespectives, Land, Sea and Sky. The realistic cinematography, limited dialogue, and powerful sound design, all together, they cohesively deliver an unforgettable experience, placing the audience directly in the middle of the chaos and fear of war
